Cup Match

Level 1 60 seconds

How to Play

1. Arrange the cups in the correct order by dragging and dropping them.

2. You have a limited amount of time to complete each level.

3. From level 13 onwards, there will be duplicate cup colors to make it more challenging.

4. Once you are satisfied with your arrangement, click the 'Check Arrangement' button to see if you are correct.

5. If correct, you'll move on to the next level. If not, try again!
