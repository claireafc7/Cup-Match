Welcome to the Game

How to Play

1. The goal of the game is to arrange the cups in the correct order.

2. Drag and drop the cups to rearrange them.

3. You have a limited amount of time to complete each level.

4. Starting from level 13, there will be duplicate cup colors to increase difficulty.

5. Check your arrangement by pressing the 'Check Arrangement' button.

6. If your arrangement is correct, you'll move on to the next level.
