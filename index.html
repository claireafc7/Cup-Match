Cup Match

Level 1 60 seconds

How to Play

1. The cups need to be arranged in a specific order.

2. You can arrange the cups in the correct order by dragging and dropping them.

3. You have a limited amount of time to complete each level.

4. Once you are satisfied with your arrangement, click the 'Check Arrangement' button to see if you are correct.

5. If correct, you'll move on to the next level. If not, move some cups and try again!
